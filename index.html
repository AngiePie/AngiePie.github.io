About Me

Hello, I'm Angelina!

As a high school junior in Oakland, California, I'm passionate about using design and technology to solve real-world problems. My journey in tech began with a desire to create tools that can make a difference in people's lives.

Through my work with Project Invent and GameHeads, I've developed a strong foundation in project management, product design, and game development. I enjoy leading teams and bringing creative ideas to life through collaboration and iteration.

My experience includes developing a video game called "Sneaky Fishy," creating a smart calendar for older adults, and designing an electronic drill bit organizer for teachers. These projects have taught me the importance of empathy, user feedback, and perseverance in the design process.

When I'm not working on tech projects, I enjoy volunteering at my local church and exploring new creative tools and techniques. I'm constantly looking for opportunities to learn and grow, and I'm excited to continue developing my skills in design, coding, and leadership.

My goal is to pursue a career that combines my technical skills with my passion for making a positive impact in my community. I believe that technology should be accessible to everyone, and I'm committed to creating solutions that address real needs and improve people's lives.
